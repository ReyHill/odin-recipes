Pizza Recipe

About this Recipe

This recipe will provide you with the knowledge needed to prepare a pepperoni pizza!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Water
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Add yeast to a bowl with hot water
  2. Once yeast blooms, take out flour
  3. Mix the yeast, water, and flour mixture until it makes dough
  4. Knead the dough with a roller
  5. Shape the dough like a pizza
  6. Put sauce on the dough, then add cheese and pepperoni
  7. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  8. Cook for 15-20 minutes, let it cool, then serve!
